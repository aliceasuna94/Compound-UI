Settle in any asset supported by the protocol

Problem

OTC trading desks serve their clients and counterparties by enabling them to frictionlessly trade in size; this requires OTC desks to constantly re-balance their inventory across a range of volatile assets, in order to respond to incoming trade requests.

Solution

By holding stablecoins in Compound, OTC traders have instant borrowing access to any asset that the Compound protocol supports, with minimal exposure to price volatility. They can settle trades in any of these assets immediately, and repay borrowed assets at anytime.
